# MYSION - Global Christian Community

## About MYSION

**MYSION.CO** is our most ambitious platform to connect Christian communities globally through purpose-driven technology.

### Current status
- **Active members**: 350+
- **Languages**: Spanish and English (multilingual plans)
- **Status**: Functional, seeking funding

### Vision

Build a global Christian social network that:
- Connects faith communities around the world
- Facilitates sharing resources and experiences
- Promotes impact projects with purpose
- Supports multiple widely spoken languages
